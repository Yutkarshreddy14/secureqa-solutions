How It Works

Our QA process,
start to finish

A structured workflow designed to keep testing clear, organised, and transparent.

01

Requirement Analysis

We review product requirements, workflows, and expected behaviour to understand what needs to be tested and how.

02

Test Planning

We prepare testing scope, scenarios, and execution strategy — shared before work begins so you know exactly what's covered.

03

Test Execution

Testing across features, browsers, devices, and user flows. Every case logged with pass/fail status and context.

04

Bug Reporting

Clear issue reporting with reproduction steps, screenshots, severity levels, and environment details filed in your preferred tool.

05

Retesting

Verification of fixes and regression checks to confirm resolved issues are fully closed and nothing new was introduced.

06

Final QA Summary

A clean summary of issues found, fixes verified, and testing coverage — ready to share with your team or stakeholders.
